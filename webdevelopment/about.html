Reed Esslinger

Educator Credentials

My name is Reed Esslinger, and I am a network engineer with 17 years of experience. I have written Guides, RCAs, and runbooks for technical analysis. I designed learning modules for technical applications, managed services, and created a support operations standardization specification. I managed projects, implemented changes, investigated high-level outages in a 3-tiered environment, and utilized common and uncommon tools in the administration of networked computing environments. I have experience with both commercial off-the-shelf hardware and software, as well as specialized applications in an enterprise environment. I have over 3 years of experience in network-cloud operations, including deployment and design. I have worked with the most notable companies in the industry including IBM and Microsoft.

I have 10 industry certifications, including an A+, Network+, Security+, Cloud+, CySA+, AZ900, ITIL Foundations and a bachelor's degree in information systems. I'm currently working on a Masters in Instructional Design. This site is a presentation of some of my work in the educational technology space.
